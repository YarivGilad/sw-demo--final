Service Worker Lifecycle: When a service worker is registered, it goes through a lifecycle of events: installing, installed, activating, and activated. A key aspect of this lifecycle is that a service worker will only start controlling pages (i.e., be able to intercept fetch requests and manage caching behavior) after it becomes active. Even when it becomes active, it only controls pages that are loaded after its activation. Pages that were already loaded before the service worker activated will not be controlled by it until they are reloaded.
